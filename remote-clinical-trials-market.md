<p><p>Remote clinical trials encompass various market types, including interventional, observational, and expanded access trials. Interventional trials involve active intervention by researchers to evaluate drug efficacy or treatment protocols. Observational trials, on the other hand, study participants in a natural setting without intervention, focusing on health outcomes and behaviors. Expanded access trials allow patients with serious conditions to access investigational therapies outside usual clinical trials, aiming to provide treatment options when standard therapies lack effectiveness or availability.</p></p>
