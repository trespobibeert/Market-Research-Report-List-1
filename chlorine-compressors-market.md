<p><strong>The Chlorine Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Ring Compressors</li><li>Centrifugal Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chlorine compressors market primarily includes liquid ring compressors and centrifugal compressors, each serving distinct operational needs. Liquid ring compressors utilize a liquid film to create compression, offering reliable performance for handling corrosive gases like chlorine. They are efficient at low flow rates and can manage variable loads. Centrifugal compressors, on the other hand, are designed for high-flow applications and provide consistent pressure through high-speed rotation, making them suitable for large-scale chlorine processing in industrial settings.</p></p>
